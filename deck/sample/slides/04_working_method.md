# The working method

- **Branches instead of a shared canvas** — threads are isolated while
  working; reconciliation is a *protocol agents can execute* (fixed
  per-file-class merge rules + audits), not an accident to untangle
- **Markdown and HTML are the source** — office formats are generated
  outputs, never hand-edited sources
- **Edit by critique, not by hand** — describe what's wrong and what you
  want; the agent improves on it and runs the gates
- **Composed prompts, not dictation** — draft email-length instructions in
  an editor, paste them in; the prompt is the first draft of the work

<!-- notes -->
Four commitments, one theme: keep the human at the level of intent and let
the machinery handle propagation.
